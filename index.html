About Us

Learn more about the history and purpose of CyberTalk '24, and why it stands as a premier event for students across the nation.

Our Journey

CyberTalk '24 is a premier national-level technical symposium designed for college students across the country. Since 2003, CyberTalk has provided a platform for young innovators to showcase their skills in cutting-edge technologies. From coding competitions to expert talks, we offer a unique experience for participants to expand their knowledge and network with industry leaders. Join us in shaping the future of technology!

Venue & Schedule

Venue: Department of MCA, Sona College of Technology, Salem.

Date: 26th September 2024 (Thursday).

Mark your calendars and be ready to immerse yourself in a day filled with learning, collaboration, and innovation. Our venue offers the perfect environment to engage in this enriching experience.

Our Mission

At CyberTalk, our mission is to cultivate a culture of innovation and creativity among students. We aim to provide a platform where bright minds can come together to solve real-world challenges and contribute to the advancement of technology and cybersecurity.

Our Vision

We envision a future where students are empowered to lead the charge in technological advancements. Our goal is to inspire the next generation of tech leaders and innovators through comprehensive knowledge-sharing and collaboration opportunities.

General Information

Please review the general information provided for participants to ensure a smooth and informed experience.

  • UG students from Arts and Science background are invited.
  • Registration fee per participant is Rs.200/-
  • Participants can participate in multiple events in a single registration
  • Each member of the team must be individually registered.
  • Participants must wear their college ID card as proof of identity.
  • Entry begins at 8:00 AM.
  • A registration kit, refreshment, and lunch will be provided.
  • Participant certificates will be provided as e-certificates.
  • Winner and runner-up prizes will be awarded.
